Bandeja Paisa

The Paisa Tray is probably the most popular dish in Colombia, it is originally from the Andean region of the country, where people are called "Paisas" and the area where I was born and raised.


Paisa tray is something I have eaten all my life, and if I had to choose my last meal on this earth, this is the dish I would choose without a doubt. Traditionally, the Paisa Tray includes beans, white rice, pork rinds, meat powder, chorizo, fried egg, ripe plantain, avocado, and arepa, but you can substitute meat powder for grilled beef or pork. I usually make the beans and ground beef the day before to make the recipe easier.


INGREDIENTS


  • Antioquian Beans
  • White rice
  • meat powder
  • Greaves
  • Cooked Sausages
  • Fried eggs
  • Banana slices
  • Avocado
  • Home

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the beans, hogao and powdered meat the day before and store in the fridge.
  2. When you are ready to serve the paisa tray, heat the beans, hogao, the meat powder and prepare the pork rinds.
  3. Cook white rice and plantains. Fry the eggs and sausages.
